The Trip of a Lifetime:
Australia and New Zealand

Discover places unlike any other on Earth! Australia and New Zealand are well-known for their unique landscapes, wildlife, and indigenous cultures, as well as their vibrant cities.

Whether you’re looking for a beach vacation, an outdoor adventure, an au pair opportunity, or simply a chance to improve your English in the sun with the friendliest of locals, look no further than Australia and New Zealand.

Take a road trip along Australia’s Great Ocean Road or get your adrenaline pumping with bungee jumping, jet boating, and skydiving in New Zealand’s adventure sports capital, Queenstown.

There truly is something for everyone.

Explore Down under

Australia offers a diverse and unique travel experience that attracts millions of visitors every year. Here are some reasons why you should consider visiting Australia:

NATURAL BEAUTY

Australia is home to some of the world's most stunning landscapes and wonders of nature. Its diverse ecosystems range from tropical rainforests to arid deserts, providing opportunities for outdoor enthusiasts and nature lovers. Spots to cross off the bucket list include the Great Barrier Reef, Uluru (Ayers Rock), the Twelve Apostles, and the Blue Mountains.

UNIQUE WILDLIFE

Australia is home to a variety of unique animals, such as kangaroos, koalas, wallabies, and wombats. The country also has a rich marine life. You can’t miss out on the Great Barrier Reef, which is home to thousands of species of fish, coral, and other marine creatures

BEACHES

With over 10,000 beaches, Australia has a coastline to suit every traveler's taste, from pristine white sands to rugged cliffs and surfing hotspots.

CULTURAL EXPERIENCE

Australian cities are known for their vibrant arts and cultural scenes, with numerous museums, galleries, and festivals throughout the year. Indigenous Australian culture and history are also an essential part of the country's heritage and can be experienced through various cultural centers and tours
